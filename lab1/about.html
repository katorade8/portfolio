ABOUT ME

My name is Kade, but my mom calls me a knucklehead. I'm a landscape photographer out of Provo!

I'm passionate about sports and the outdoors. Good games of volleyball and basketball are some of the most enjoyable things in this life. I also love to go backpacking and canyoneering - sometimes both in the same trip!

I started taking pictures as soon as I had a phone with a decent camera. Since then, I try to take amazing pictures of places I go to remind myself how beautiful this world really is.

Thanks for visiting my KG Photography!
